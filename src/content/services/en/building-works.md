## Building Works & Restoration Services

**PA.VE.SA. srl** offers comprehensive **civil and industrial construction** services, combining traditional building craftsmanship with modern restoration and protective technologies. Utilizing our own autonomous mobile equipment, we carry out external and internal renovations, facade restorations, and rehabilitation programs on any building type.

From industrial protective coatings to micro-sandblasting of delicate historical surfaces, we deliver turn-key solutions to preserve and enhance your real estate assets.

---

### Our Range of Construction Interventions

*   **Painting & Coatings**: Internal and external painting for residential, commercial, and industrial structures. We use high-resistance siloxane, quartz, or acrylic coatings designed to withstand severe weather conditions.
*   **Concrete Repair & Restoration**: Professional removal of spalled concrete, rust passivation of exposed steel reinforcement bars, and volumetric reconstruction using high-strength thixotropic non-shrink mortar.
*   **Facade & Exposed Masonry Restoration**: Deep cleaning and pollution stain removal using controlled pressure wet blasting or micro-sandblasting, ideal for bricks and natural stone.
*   **Interior Finishing, Plastering, & Drywall**: Skimming, partitioning, suspended ceilings, and gypsum plaster board installations for perfectly flat and ready-to-finish surfaces.

---

### Protective Treatments & Remediation

> [!TIP]
> **Anti-Humidity & Mold Solutions**: We apply full thermal insulation and anti-mold systems to permanently eliminate thermal bridges and rising damp issues.

*   **Terrace & Balcony Waterproofing**: High-performance, elastic waterproofing membranes applied directly over existing tiles without demolition.
*   **Water-Repellent Sealing for Stone & Brick**: Application of invisible siloxane sealers that prevent water absorption while allowing the masonry to breathe.
*   **Biocidal Disinfection**: Certified treatments to eradicate algae, mold, and moss from walls before new painting cycles.
