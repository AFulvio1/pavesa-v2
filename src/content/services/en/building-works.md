## Building Works

Equipped with a mobile sandblasting system, our company is able to perform restoration and provide treatments for buildings and everything related to construction. Through micro-sandblasting, accurate cleaning of beams, wooden attics, and general masonry is achieved.

**PA.VE.SA. srl** scrapes and cleans masonry from falling plaster, sandblasts concrete, bricks, stones, and exposed marble. In addition, we offer professional waterproofing treatments against mold and weathering.

On external facades, we adopt the hydro-sandblasting technique to greatly reduce the production of light dust and ensure respect for the environment and urban contexts.

### Our building intervention services

- **Painting** - Civil and industrial painting with high-quality products resistant to atmospheric agents.
- **Wall smoothing and finishing** - Accurate preparation of surfaces for a smooth and aesthetically flawless finish.
- **Plaster and masonry restoration** - Removal of degraded plaster and rebuilding of masonry sections.
- **Anti-mold and protective treatments** - Application of long-lasting protective solutions against humidity, algae, and mold.
- **Facade cleaning and restoration** - Removal of smog, graffiti, and signs of aging through pressure-controlled hydro-sandblasting.
